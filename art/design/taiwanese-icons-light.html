TAIWANESE ICONS REMIXED


Bao Li Da (保力達B) is a local drink that is a combination of chinese medicine and alcohol [~10%]. It tastes kind of like an earthy cough syrup with too much sugar. My gym trainer said it will pickle your insides slightly differently than normal alcohol. I prefer mixing it with a Gold Medal Taiwan beer, but others recommend root beer soda or green tea.

I love the design of the brand/ bottle and wanted to make an homage to it in the form of wearable clothing.

This is a clear case of copyright infringement, so you can't buy them. However I will be happy to send you one in the mail in exchange for something interesting.

Betel nut boxes are ubiquitous. I've seen enough instagram galleries curating these; I won't pretend like I was the first person to highlight them in a hipsterish way. I do feel like these would be a hit being sold at a night market or tourist spot though.
